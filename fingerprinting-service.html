What is Fingerprinting?

Fingerprinting is the process of recording an individual's unique fingerprint patterns for identification purposes. It is commonly used for background checks, employment verification, and legal documentation. Our fingerprinting services comply with all state and federal regulations to ensure accuracy and security.
